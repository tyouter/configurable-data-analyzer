The hybrid approach: AI + Local Processing

What makes Rednote Data Analyzer different is how it combines two types of intelligence:

🧠

AI (Cloud)

Understands your question in plain English, figures out which metric you want, and decides how to calculate it — like a smart assistant that knows about KPIs and data analysis.

⚡

Python (Local)

Does the actual number-crunching — loads Excel files, filters rows, counts unique users, calculates averages — all running on your machine, keeping your data private and secure.

💡
Key Insight

This hybrid pattern is why the tool works so well. You ask questions the way you naturally would, and the AI figures out the technical details while the local code handles the heavy data processing. It's like having a data analyst who also speaks your language.
